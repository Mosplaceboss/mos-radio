# Staff Guide

This guide is for on-air staff using Mo's Place Studio in **Staff Mode**.

## Your main screen

Open **Daily Operations**. Everything you need for a normal shift is on one page.

## What you can do

- Refresh station status
- See who is on air and what is playing
- Review alerts
- Open RadioDJ (with confirmation)
- Run News Now (with confirmation)
- Restart approved watchers (with confirmation)

## What is hidden in Staff Mode

Staff Mode hides technical tools on purpose:

- Platform paths and folder editors
- Git and development information
- Raw JSON configuration
- Inventory, deployment, and update tools
- Advanced automation screens

If you need a technical change, contact the station owner or engineer.

## Before your shift

1. Open Daily Operations and refresh.
2. Confirm service lights are green or yellow with a clear message.
3. Check the next scheduled event.
4. Review today's advertising notes.

## During your shift

Use Daily Operations to monitor status. Only restart watchers or run news when station policy allows and Studio asks you to confirm.

## After your shift

No special shutdown is required. Studio saves your last screen automatically.

## Getting help

Open **Help** in Studio or ask the station owner to switch to Owner or Advanced Mode for deeper troubleshooting.
